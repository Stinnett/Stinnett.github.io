About

I'm an NRC Research Fellow at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in the Nuclear, Plasma, and Radiological Engineering (NPRE) department, defending my PhD in early fall 2016.

Education

I received my Bachelor's degrees in Physics and Mathematics from the University of Oklahoma in 2012. I finished my Master's in NPRE at UIUC in 2014 and am on track to finish my PhD this fall.

Research Interests

In general, I'm interested in algorithmic decision making, whether it's applied to isotope identification (like my current research), to personal loan predictions (see my DS demo project), to medical diagnoses, or elsewhere.

Outside the Lab

I compete in amateur strongman and previously in powerlifting (when grad school allows).
